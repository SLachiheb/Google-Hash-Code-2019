Modeling assistance in CPLEX V12.7.0

Announces modeling assistance as a new feature of CPLEX V12.7.0.

CPLEX V12.7.0 offers assistance to help you to identify and to eliminate troublesome elements from your model. Turn on modeling assistance by setting the data consistency checking and modeling assistance parameter to the value CPX_DATACHECK_ASSIST (2). After this data-checking parameter is turned on at this level, CPLEX modeling assistance prints warnings (in the warning log or on the warning channel, depending on your settings for messages). These warnings become available as CPLEX solves your model; that is, they are not yet available as CPLEX reads your model. The warnings are about potential problems in your model. For example, modeling assistance will report bounds, coefficients, and righthand side (RHS) values that are inappropriately large or small.

For more detail about modeling assistance, see the new topic Modeling assistance in CPLEX in the CPLEX User's Manual. That new topic also directs you to new modeling information symbols that appear in reports of modeling assistance.
